MAHJONG AT MARA'S

THE FAMILIES YOU ARE BORN INTO AND THE FAMILIES YOU CHOOSE

Seventeen years after losing her soulmate on 9/11, Lila, a nurse's aide, and her teenage son Dante find comfort in unexpected friends—a group of Mahjong-playing octogenarians.

MAHJONG AT MARA'S is a story about Suburban New Jersey, the classic example of a patchwork community. Nearby neighborhoods made up of people from different ethnicities and economic backgrounds—meeting at the local gas station, grocery store, and café—exchanging brief moments of politeness, both sincere and forced. People we rarely imagine sharing much more than small talk with … until we do.
